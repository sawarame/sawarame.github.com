# Feature Overview

Explore the various organization and customization features available in TabOrigami.

## Flexible Organization Styles

Choose the best categorization style to match your current workflow:

- **Auto**: Let the AI automatically determine the best categories.
- **Task Focus**: Group tabs by ongoing tasks or projects.
- **Work-Life**: Clearly separate business-related tabs from personal ones.
- **Triage**: Easily organize unnecessary tabs to keep your browser clutter-free.
- **Custom (Prompt)**: Provide custom text instructions to the AI for personalized grouping.

## Rename and Recolor Groups

You can freely modify the group names and colors suggested by the AI before executing.

- On the preview screen, click a suggested group name to edit it directly.
- Use the custom color picker to choose the color for the tab group as it will appear in Chrome.
- The names and colors you set will be directly applied to the actual Chrome tab groups once executed.

## Exclude Pinned Tabs

This feature protects important tabs you always keep open (pinned tabs).
By enabling this setting, pinned tabs will be excluded from the organization process and remain in their original positions.

## Smart Cleanup

When organizing tabs, TabOrigami automatically detects and closes redundant "New Tab" pages.
Additionally, tabs from the same domain are recognized as highly related and are grouped together efficiently.
