## How to use

1. Select or drag & drop the photo file (primarily JPEG) you want to check.
2. After selection, the analysis starts automatically, and metadata such as shooting date, camera model, settings, and GPS will be displayed.
3. Click the copy button next to each item to copy the value to your clipboard.
4. If GPS information is available, you can click the link to view the shooting location on Google Maps.
5. Click the clear button (trash can icon) to reset the displayed information.
6. From the link in the analysis result card, you can pass the image directly to the [Image Resizer & Cropper](/resize) to remove EXIF information or resize it.

:::info Privacy
🔒 Photo analysis is performed entirely on your device (in-browser), and no data is ever sent to an external server. Your privacy is fully protected.
:::
