## How to use

1. Select the images you want to optimize by dragging and dropping or clicking (multiple selection supported). Pasting from clipboard (Ctrl+V) is also supported.
2. If needed, you can crop each image using the 'Crop' button. Preset ratios for social media are available.
3. Adjust the output format (WebP recommended), maximum width, and quality from the 'Optimization Settings'. You can choose to preserve EXIF data for JPEG format.
4. Click 'Start Optimization' to process the images within your browser.
5. After processing, use the 'Compare' button to check the image quality, and click 'Download' to save the images. If there are multiple images, they will be downloaded as a ZIP file.

:::info Privacy & Security
🔒 Image optimization and cropping are performed entirely on your device (in-browser). Your data is never sent to any external server, making it safe for confidential images.
:::
