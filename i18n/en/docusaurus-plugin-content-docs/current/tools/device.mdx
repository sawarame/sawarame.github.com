## Displayed Information

- **Network**: Shows your current public IP address.
- **Screen Size**:
  - **Physical Resolution**: Total screen pixels retrieved from `window.screen`.
  - **Window Size**: Current browser viewport size (`window.innerWidth/Height`).
- **Hardware**:
  - **CPU Cores**: Number of logical CPU cores.
  - **Memory Capacity**: Approximate RAM capacity (may be limited due to browser privacy protections).
- **User Agent**: The identification string sent by your browser.

:::info Privacy
🔒 Except for retrieving your IP address, all information is obtained directly within your browser. No device information is stored on our servers.
:::
