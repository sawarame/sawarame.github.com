## How to use

1. Select the type of information you want to encode (URL, Wi-Fi, Contact, etc.).
2. Enter the required details; the preview will update in real-time.
3. Optionally, add a logo to the center of your QR code using presets or by uploading your own image.
4. Choose your preferred resolution (240px / 480px) and click "Save QR" to download.
5. Use the "Share" button to send the QR code to other apps or copy it to your clipboard.

:::info Privacy
🔒 All text inputs and logo images are processed entirely within your browser and are never sent to any server.
:::
