🎯How to Use

  1. Enter items you want to draw into the 'Items List' one per line. You can also import from a text file.
  2. If you turn on 'Remove after draw', the won item will be automatically removed from the list.
  3. Press the 'Start Draw' button to spin the roulette and pick one item randomly.
  4. The 'History' section shows previous results.
🔒 Your data and history are only saved in your browser's local storage and are never sent to any server. You can safely use it even for sensitive information.
